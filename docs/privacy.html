Privacy Policy

Rewire: Addiction Recovery · Effective July 11, 2026

Rewire is built so that your recovery journey stays private. This policy explains, in plain language, what data the app handles and what it never does.

The short version

  • No account. You never sign up, and we never ask for your name, email, or phone number.
  • Your data stays on your device. Streaks, check-ins, quiz answers, motivations, journal photos, and reminders are stored locally on your iPhone only.
  • We do not sell data. We have nothing to sell — your personal content never reaches our servers.

Data stored on your device

Rewire keeps everything it needs in a local file inside the app's own storage, protected by iOS file encryption. This includes your streak history, daily check-ins, onboarding quiz answers, motivations, appearance-tracker photos, reminder settings, and progress badges. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself using the Data Backup feature.

Face ID

If you turn on app lock, Face ID / Touch ID checks happen entirely through Apple's on-device framework. Rewire never sees, stores, or transmits biometric data.

Notifications

Daily reminders are scheduled locally on your device. No notification content is sent to or from any server.

Analytics

Rewire may collect anonymous usage analytics (for example, which screens are opened or whether onboarding was completed) to help us improve the app. If enabled, this is limited strictly to feature-usage events. It never includes personal content — no quiz answers, no check-in details, no relapse information, no motivations, and no photos. Analytics contain no name, email, or contact details.

Purchases

Any purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details.

Deleting your data

Because everything lives on your device, deleting the app deletes all of your data. There is nothing to request from us, because we hold nothing.

Children

Rewire is intended for users aged 17 and over and is not directed at children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new effective date. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
